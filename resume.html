Summary

These days, I get excited about two things: Leading teams and bringing great design to life.

My background as a Front-End Developer over the past 8 years includes work with Drupal, Wordpress, Ruby on Rails, Shopify, Jekyll, Squarespace and custom applications in a variety of languages. I have worked in industries ranging from healthcare (Pearlpoint, UrgentTeam, QHR) to businesses (Dave Ramsey, Tony Robbins, OHL) to entertainment (CMT, Lady Antebellum, Brad Paisley, Dolly Parton) and more.

I proudly consider myself a communicating developer as I am at my best when involved in strategy and training both with product owners and fellow team members. I have experience doing both in person and via remote communication.

Although I am a life-long learner, I specialize in the following languages/processes: HTML5, CSS3/SASS, Javascript/jQuery/Coffeescript, PHP, Git/Github, Grunt/Gulp/Yeoman/Bower and LAMP stack development. Lately, I've been learning React and creating an automated living styleguide using Gulp/Jekyll.

Oh, and I believe whole-heartedly in the following: exercise, regular breaks, and not taking yourself too seriously.
